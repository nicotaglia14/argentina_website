Iguazu Falls

Located in the province of Misiones, Iguazu Falls is a massive group of different waterfalls. Walk across the main trails to see the big waterfalls, get deeper into the jungle and discover hidden small waterfalls, go on the helicopter ride that takes you extremely close to the waterfalls, go on the small boat that takes you where the waterfall breaks to feel the power of the massive waterfalls. Do all of those things or anything you decide, but enjoy the marvelous things this unique place has to offer.

Quebrada de Humahuaca

The Quebrada de Humahuaca can be found in the province of Jujuy. This region has plenty of small towns where tourists can go learn a lot about a new culture. If you like walking, there are lots of trecking activities in the mountains to get to the peaks and take some wonderful pictures. The main attraction of this region is the Cerro de Los Siete Colores: a colorful hill that attracts people from all over the world.

Perito Moreno Glacier

Have some winter clothes and need to use them? Visit the Perito Moreno glacier in the province of Santa Cruz. Most people choose to do walks around the surrounding mountains to get a view as shown in the picture. However, other people choose to get closer to the glacier and be able to watch when gigantic pieces of ice fall from the main glacier causing big waves and a super loud noise.

Mendoza Province

One of the most complete provinces in Argentina is Mendoza. Mountains, rivers, cities, and more are located in Mendoza. The highest peak in South America, the Aconcagua, can be found there. If you like adrenaline, doing rafting across the Atuel river is a very popular activity apart from trying to reach the peak of the Aconcagua. If you need to relax a little bit after rafting or trecking, visit the vineyards and factories where wine is made to learn about it and tried some of the best wine in the world.

Peninsula de Valdes

All of the animal lovers decide to visit Peninsula de Valdes in Chubut province. Lots of penguins can be seen in the coast where tourists have access to. There are also boats that go into the sea and get you close to the whales where you can have close encounter to this huge sea animals.

El Chalten

Heaven on Earth is what tourists have named this place in Argentina. El Chalten is located in the province of Santa Cruz at the south of Argentina. This place combines crystal clear lakes with snowy mountains. The Cerro Fitz Roy attracts alpinists because of the high level skills required to reach the peak of this extremely hard mountain. However, if you are not an alpinist, el Chalten is still a place for you. The mountains and lakes can be also seen from the bottom of the mountains and they are still amazing.

Ischigualasto Provincial Park

If you have some time at the west side of Argentina, Ischigualasto Provincial Park is where the Valley Moon is found. This desert contains rocks that seem to defy gravity and places where we can connect to the past by watching real fossils from dinosaurs. This place not only received that name because of the moon-shaped rocks that can be seen, but also because of how bright the moon can be seen during the nights at this place due to the lack of light and air pollution. So, if you would like to have a relaxing time in a calm place, do not hesitate to visit Ischigualasto Provincial Park in San Juan province.

Tren a las Nubes

Some people say that trains are boring. Those people have probably not taken a ride in the Tren A Las Nubes. This special train can be found in the province of Salta. Tren A Las Nubes is translated as "train to the clouds" and it receives this name because it goes across the mountains and valleys at an incredible height. This is a unique place in the world and a wonderful place to get some good pictures while having a good time.

Nahuel Huapi National Park

Nahuel Huapi National park is located in Bariloche at the province of Rio Negro. This area contains some crystal clear lakes where activities such as kayaking, swimming, snorkeling, and more can be done. There are also small boats that will take you to different islands to discover different but all wonderful places. If you like urban legends, you can try your luck trying to find Nahuelito, the monster that lives in this lakes!
