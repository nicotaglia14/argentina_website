El Ateneo

Once a theatre, now a library, El Ateneo is a place you must visit if you are in the province of Buenos Aires. Its unique infrastructure and the amount of books it has to offer makes this place attractive for people from all over the world. Even if you are not interested in books at all, this place is a good place to just walk around and see what you can find.

Puerto Madero

Puerto Madero is a port found in Buenos Aires, Argentina. This place is an awesome choice for tourists who want to have a good time and enjoy a delicious dinner. Enjoy the marvelous views and the architecture of the Puente De La Mujer, a strangely-shaped bridge that people like to visit. You can also take pictures during the night when the colorful lights brigthen it up. After all the walking, you may be hungry, but you are in the right place for that. Puerto Madero has a lot of restaurants where tourists can stop by to have an awesome dinner with a great view.

Galerias Pacifico

Galerias Pacifico is a unique shopping mall located in Buenos Aires. This shopping mall was created on the 18th of May in 1992 and, since that day, people from all over the world stop by to buy clothes, get some coffee or just walk around to admire its wonderful architecture. There are beautiful paintings that can be seen on the ceiling of the mall.

Boca Juniors

Boca Juniors is the biggest football team in Argentina, and its stadium, La Bombonera, is a place everyone should go. The football games are usually at night, and this is the time when the streets get the blue and gold color that represents this team. Going to one of Boca's games is a once in a lifetime experience. The cheering of the fans does not stop throughout the game. It will seem as if the stadium is jumping because of all of the excited fans cheering causes the whole stadium to shake.

Galileo Galilei Planetarium

Apart from the awesome day activities , the Galileo Galilei Planetarium offers fun night activities too. If you go inside the building, you will be able to see the stars and maybe the moon from the planetarium. However, if you prefer to stay outside, the planetarium will start shining with beautiful lights at night and even sometimes a laser show.

Parrilla Don Julio

Argentina is famous around the world for its meat, and it is a tradition for Argentinean people to eat a meal called Asado. This kind of meal is any preferred meat cooked in a certain way. It is really easy to make for Argentineans, but it is hard to get it to taste perfectly, so if you want to have the best experience eating this meal, you should go to Parrilla Don Julio. This restaurant is rated as the best restaurant of Argentina to eat Asado. Also, if you are lucky, you may meet some famous people such as Lionel Messi eating there.

Obelisk

Doing the day activities at the obelisk can be really fun. However, the obelisk is a place that represents the culture of Argentina. Going to this place at night has to be the best nigth activity you can do. The best thing about this, is that no one can tell you what to do. The best way to enjoy the obelisk is just going there and enjoying yourself. Wandering around the region during the night might lead you to a bar, a restaurant, a party, a museum, a concert or many other possible options that all happen in the region of the obelisk. The recommended activity for the obelisk would be just go and enjoy it. Have fun, wander around, walk, dance, do whatever you want, but live your best Argentinean experience.
